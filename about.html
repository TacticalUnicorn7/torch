About Me

My name is David and I am the owner of Melting Torch Studios. My dream is to produce a video with 100,000 views.

I would like to become an Architect or a Mechanic someday, but I have a long way to get there.

I am currently a member of the International Tang Soo Do Federation (ITF) and that is where I train My Martial Art.

As of right now, I am the World Champion in Forms (ages 13 to 14) and the 2 time National Champion in Forms and Sparring (ages 12 to 14).
